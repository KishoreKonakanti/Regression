Benergy Lighting Services

\n

Benergy specializes in the supply, installation, and maintenance, of quality energy efficient lighting. We provide lighting design and installation consultation for municipal, industrial, retail and home lighting projects. Benergy has a solid reputation for customer service and quality products, we will always work within your budget to achieve the best results!
